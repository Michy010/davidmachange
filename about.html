About Hon. David Machange

  • Home

Public Health Leader & Academic Administrator

Hon. David Machange is a distinguished Tanzanian public health expert and academic leader with a career spanning over two decades in healthcare policy, university administration, and national leadership.

His tenure as Minister of Health (2020-2021) during the challenging pandemic years established him as a decisive leader capable of navigating complex public health crises while maintaining transparency and public trust.

At Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Sciences (MUHAS), his progressive leadership as Vice President (2021-2022) and later as President (2024-2025) transformed the institution's research capabilities and community impact.

Leadership Philosophy

My approach to leadership is rooted in three fundamental principles that have guided my career in both public service and academia:

"True leadership is measured not by the power one wields, but by the lives one elevates and the systems one improves for generations to come."

Servant Leadership: I believe leadership exists to serve others - whether students, patients, or citizens. Every decision must answer the question: "How does this improve lives?"

Evidence-Based Action: In healthcare and education, we must balance data with compassion, letting research guide our strategies while remembering the human stories behind every statistic.

Sustainable Systems: Real progress comes from building institutions and frameworks that outlast any individual leader, creating platforms for future innovators to stand upon.
