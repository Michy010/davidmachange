About Hon. David Machange

Hon. David Machange is a distinguished public health leader and academic administrator with a proven track record of transformative leadership in both government and higher education sectors.

2024-2025
President
Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Sciences (MUHAS)
2021-2022
Vice President
Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Sciences (MUHAS)
2020-2021
Minister of Health
Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Science

With expertise spanning healthcare policy, academic administration, and institutional leadership, Hon. Machange is passionate about developing sustainable solutions to Africa's health challenges and mentoring the next generation of leaders.
